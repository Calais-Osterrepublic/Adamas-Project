mission "Adamas: Almach"
	name "Investigate <planet>"
	description "A man wants you to transport a very large cargo container to <destination>, beyond a wormhole."
	cargo "very large crate" 4
	source
		near "Almach" 1 4
	waypoint "Humaver Asas Grak"
	destination "Biotite Station"
	to offer
		has "kestrel available"
		"reputation: Syndicate" >= 1
		random < 72
	on offer
		require "Jump Drive"
		conversation
			`As you are walking through the spaceport, a man standing in front of a warehouse signals you over to him. "I've got a job for you. I need you to ferry these weapons to Deadman's Cove," he says to you, opening a door and pointing at a stack of crates in the dim light.`
			`	"Deadman's Cove?" you ask. "Isn't that a pirate planet?" He walks inside to show you the paperwork, and you follow him. He hands you a piece of paper with a few indecipherable words on it.`
			choice
				`	(Draw your weapon.)`
					goto weapon
				`	"What is this?"`
					goto ask
			label weapon
			`	As soon as you put your hand down to your gun, you hear a clicking noise and everything goes black.`
			`	Some time later, you wake up on your ship with a terrible headache no memory of the day's previous events.`
				defer
			label ask
			`	The man nods to something behind you. "I need you to transport a friend of mine beyond human space," he says. "I've heard a lot about you, Captain <last>. I think the job will be pay well enough for you." He hands you a chip with the destination loaded onto it.`
				accept
	on accept
		log "Picked up strange cargo container from <source>, as well as news of a wormhole near the Core."
	on enter
		event "cygni wormhole opens"
	on complete
		log "Dropped off a massive cargo container and discovered a new species, Adamas."
		conversation
			`You and your crew unload the crate into the back of the docking bay and you don't recieve any payment.`
